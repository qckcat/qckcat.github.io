OUR STORY

Born from Haiti. Built with Haiti.

Youth Beyond Resilience was founded in response to a painful truth: Haiti’s adolescents are growing up in neighborhoods where gangs often feel like the only path to survival, power, or belonging.

Instead of accepting that reality, Haitian leaders, educators, psychologists, and community partners came together to design something radically different — a trauma-informed ecosystem where youth can heal, create, learn, and lead.

From our first rehearsals at Holy Trinity Music School to our growing network of program centers, one conviction has guided every decision: Haitian youth are not problems to solve — they are leaders in the making. Our role is to clear the path and stand alongside them.

A Third Way Forward In communities where gangs recruit aggressively and violence feels inescapable, YBR creates a third option — safe spaces anchored in music, counseling, civic engagement, and mentorship.

WHY WE EXIST

Mission, Vision, and Values

We exist not just to keep youth away from gangs, but to build a Haiti where young people can imagine and realize futures rooted in dignity, creativity, and community leadership.

Mission

Prevent Gang Recruitment

Youth Beyond Resilience prevents gang recruitment by empowering at-risk Haitian adolescents with trauma-informed programs in mental health, arts, civic engagement, personal development, and sports.

Vision

Haitian Youth Leading Change

We envision a Haiti where every young person — regardless of circumstance — can prosper, lead, and transform their community without having to choose violence to survive.

Values

Dignity, Healing, and Shared Power

  • Dignity: Youth are protagonists, not victims.
  • Healing: Trauma-informed care in every program.
  • Partnership: Led with Haitian staff & communities.
  • Transparency: Clear, honest use of resources.
