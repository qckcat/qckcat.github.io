Who We Are

Our Story

Rooted in Haiti. Built on Resilience.

Youth Beyond Resilience was born from a simple conviction: no child should have to choose between joining a gang or living in fear. Local leaders, Haitian psychologists, and global partners came together to imagine a third path—a path defined by safety, creativity, and opportunity.

Starting with a single program center in Port-au-Prince, YBR began offering structured music, sports, and mental health support to adolescents living under daily threat of violence. What began as a pilot quickly grew into a lifeline for hundreds of youth and their families.

Today, our programs operate across three centers in gang-controlled neighborhoods, providing holistic support through our CAMPS framework. Every rehearsal, counseling session, and community project pushes back against despair and builds a culture of peace from the inside out.

Mission, Vision & Values

The Principles Guiding Our Work

Everything we do is grounded in trauma-informed practice, Haitian leadership, and an unwavering belief in the potential of youth—even in the most difficult circumstances.

Mission

Prevent Gang Recruitment

Our mission is to prevent gang recruitment by empowering at-risk Haitian adolescents with trauma-informed programs in mental health, arts, civic engagement, personal development, and sports—so they can choose hope, safety, and leadership.

Vision

A Haiti Where Youth Lead Peace

We envision a Haiti where young people are not defined by violence or poverty, but by their creativity, resilience, and capacity to transform their communities into places of safety, dignity, and opportunity.

Values

Dignity, Partnership, Courage

We honor the dignity of every young person, center Haitian leadership and local partnerships, practice financial and programmatic transparency, and choose courage over fear in the face of complex realities.

Leadership

Guided by Local Expertise & Global Partnership

YBR is led by Haitian professionals, community leaders, and an engaged board committed to financial transparency, safeguarding, and long-term impact for youth and families.

Accountability

Safeguarding Youth & Donor Resources

We believe that protecting young people and earning donor trust are non-negotiable. Our leadership team upholds strict safeguarding protocols, partner vetting, and financial controls across all program sites.

YBR tracks outcomes through pre- and post-surveys, teacher observations, and family feedback to ensure that funding is tied to real, measurable impact—not just activity.
