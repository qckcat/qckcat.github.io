Our Story

Born from urgency — built on hope.

In communities where as much as 85% of Port-au-Prince is under gang control, Haiti’s youth are pressured into impossible choices at an early age. Families are displaced, schools are interrupted, and violence becomes a daily soundtrack.

Youth Beyond Resilience was created to disrupt that trajectory. We work alongside local schools, churches, and community partners to provide safe, consistent spaces where youth can process trauma, discover their gifts, and build the skills they need to thrive.

From an initial cohort of a few dozen adolescents, we’ve grown into a multi-site initiative serving hundreds of young people every year through our CAMPS framework.

“Our vision is simple: any young person in our program should be able to say, ‘I am more than what gangs expect of me.’”

Milestones in the YBR Journey

From pilot to multi-site program
  • 2018

    Founding partners pilot trauma-informed arts and sports programming for at-risk youth in Port-au-Prince.

  • 2020

    CAMPS framework is formalized, integrating mental health, civic engagement, and personal development into all programs.

  • 2022

    Programs expand to three core centers in Croix-des-Bouquets, Delmas, and Pétion-Ville through key local partnerships.

  • 2025+

    YBR launches a five-year strategic plan to reach 1,200 youth, deepen mental health services, and document long-term outcomes.

Who We Are

Mission, Vision & Values

Every decision at Youth Beyond Resilience is filtered through a commitment to dignity, partnership, and measurable impact for Haitian youth.

Our Mission

To prevent gang recruitment by empowering at-risk Haitian adolescents with trauma-informed programs in mental health, arts, civic engagement, personal development, and sports.

Our Vision

A Haiti where young people are not defined by the violence around them, but by the resilience, talent, and leadership within them.

Our Core Values

We design every program as if a Haitian mother from our community is looking over our shoulder.

  • Co-creation
  • Safety
  • Transparency
  • Accountability
