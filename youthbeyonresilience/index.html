Our Mission

Preventing Gang Recruitment. Building Futures.

Youth Beyond Resilience prevents gang recruitment by empowering at-risk Haitian adolescents with trauma-informed programs in mental health, arts, civic engagement, personal development, and sports. We believe every young person deserves the chance to heal, to lead, and to transform their community.

The Reality

Haiti's Youth Face an Impossible Choice

In Port-au-Prince, armed groups control streets, schools, and even the walk to and from home. Adolescents are targeted because of their strength, mobility, and influence – asked to choose between joining a gang, living in fear, or leaving everything they know behind.

In some neighborhoods, an estimated 85% of territory is influenced or controlled by gangs. Without safe alternatives, everyday decisions like attending school or visiting a friend can carry real risk.

Youth Beyond Resilience exists to offer a third path – one defined by safety, healing, creativity, and leadership – so Haiti's youth can write a different story for themselves and their communities.

“When you give a young person a safe place to belong, you don't just prevent violence – you help them discover who they were always meant to become.”

Our Programs

The CAMPS Framework: Five Pillars of Transformation

CAMPS is a holistic framework that meets youth where they are – nurturing their minds, talents, and leadership potential so they can choose hope over violence.

C

Civic Engagement & Community Service

Youth lead service projects, learn about their rights, and practice building a more peaceful, just Haiti from the ground up.

Learn More →

A

Arts & Cultural Expression

Through orchestra, choir, visual arts, and Haitian drumming, youth transform trauma into creativity, dignity, and joy.

Learn More →

M

Mental Health & Wellness

Group and individual counseling, trauma-informed care, and family support tools help youth heal and regain their sense of self.

Learn More →

P

Personal Development & Empowerment

Life skills, financial literacy, and decision-making tools equip youth to lead, dream, and plan for their futures.

S

Sports & Recreation

Football for Peace and other sports programs teach teamwork, conflict resolution, and the joy of healthy competition.
