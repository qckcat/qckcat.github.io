Our Mission

Preventing Gang Recruitment. Building Futures.

Youth Beyond Resilience exists to prevent gang recruitment by empowering at-risk Haitian adolescents with trauma-informed programs in mental health, arts, civic engagement, personal development, and sports. We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to prosper, lead, and transform their community.

The Reality

Haiti's Youth Face an Impossible Choice

In Port-au-Prince, armed groups control streets, schools, and even the walk to and from home. Adolescents are targeted because of their strength and influence, pushed toward a false choice: join a gang, live in fear, or leave everything behind.

In some neighborhoods, an estimated 85% of territory is influenced or controlled by gangs. Without safe alternatives, everyday decisions like attending school or visiting a friend can carry real risk.

Youth Beyond Resilience offers a different path – one defined by safety, healing, creativity, and leadership – so Haiti's youth can build a future beyond violence.

“When you give a young person a safe place to belong, you don't just prevent violence – you help them discover who they were always meant to become.”
