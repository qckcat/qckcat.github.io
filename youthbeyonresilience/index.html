Our Mission

Preventing Gang Recruitment. Building Futures.

Youth Beyond Resilience prevents gang involvement by providing at-risk Haitian adolescents with trauma-informed programs that transform struggle into strength, fear into hope, and violence into peace.

The Reality

Haiti's Youth Face an Impossible Choice

In Port-au-Prince, where 85% of the city is controlled by gangs, children and adolescents ages 10-18 endure unimaginable circumstances. Gang recruitment is not just a risk—it's a constant, life-threatening reality.

Youth are losing hope for their future. Schools have closed. Families are displaced. The pressure to join armed groups grows stronger every day.

"Without intervention, these young people have nowhere to turn."

But there is another way. Youth Beyond Resilience offers a lifeline—programs that address trauma, develop skills, and create genuine alternatives to gang involvement.

Our Programs

The CAMPS Framework: Five Pillars of Transformation

Our holistic approach combines mental health, self-empowerment, creative activities, and community strengthening into five integrated components designed to save lives.

C

Civic Engagement

Cultivating young leaders who take pride in their Haitian identity and are prepared to contribute positively to their communities through service and advocacy.

Learn More
A

Arts & Cultural Expression

Orchestra, choir, visual arts, and Haitian drumming programs that foster creativity, discipline, and social integration through structured music and arts education.

Learn More
M

Mental Health & Wellness

Group counseling three times weekly, individual sessions for vulnerable participants, and family support tools led by Haitian psychologists who understand local trauma.

Learn More
P

Personal Development

Workshops to boost confidence, resilience, leadership, budgeting, and decision-making skills. Each participant receives a personal development workbook.

Learn More
S

Sports & Recreation

Football for Peace program promoting teamwork, conflict resolution, and community unity through structured soccer training and tournament opportunities.
