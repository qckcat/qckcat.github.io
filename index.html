The Chef

From Sardinia to San Antonio

Born and raised on the island of Sardinia, Chef Grazia Hughes grew up immersed in the traditions of Mediterranean cooking. Her earliest memories are of her grandmother's kitchen‚Äîthe warmth of fresh-baked bread, the fragrance of wild herbs, and generations gathered around the table.

After moving to America with her husband, a United States Air Force veteran, Chef Grazia brought these cherished culinary traditions to San Antonio. Here, she discovered a community that shares the Italian values of family, hospitality, and the joy of sharing exceptional food.

At COOKLANDIA, Chef Grazia creates bespoke culinary experiences that honor her Sardinian heritage while embracing the finest local ingredients. Whether preparing your family's weekly meals, orchestrating an elegant dinner party, or teaching the art of traditional pasta-making, her approach is rooted in authenticity, technique, and genuine care.

"Every dish I prepare carries the soul of my homeland and is crafted with the same devotion I would give my own family."
