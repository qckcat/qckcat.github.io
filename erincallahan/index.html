The Journey

The Roots

Born in Duncanville, Texas, Erin moved to San Antonio on New Year's Eve 1988 as a freshly-minted 10-year-old. From playing in the creek to inventing broom ball on skates during rainy days, creativity was woven into every childhood moment.

The Foundation

After graduating from Incarnate Word High School, Erin earned her BBA in Marketing from the University of Texas at Austin. For twenty years, she built expertise in marketing strategy, education, and creative problem-solving.

The Spark

During COVID, art supplies overtook the house. A playful argument with her husband Patrick led to a simple request: "Why don't you just build me an art studio in the backyard?" That question changed everything.

When Creativity Meets Construction

Neighbors started noticing. Questions turned into conversations. Conversations turned into a vision. When Patrick suggested "elbow room" and Erin added "some," they knew they had something special. In April 2020, Some Elbow Room was born‚Äînot from a business plan, but from a genuine need for creative space and the realization that everyone deserves room to pursue their passions.
