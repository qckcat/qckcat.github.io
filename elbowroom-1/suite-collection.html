Suite Collection.

Accessory structures with full bathrooms and optional kitchenettes—guest suites and casitas built for real comfort, privacy, and long-term flexibility.

Structure type: Accessory Structures w/ Plumbing · Typical range: $75k–$140k

Room for their bags, not just their toothbrush.

Suite models are designed to keep guests comfortable for a weekend or a season. That means full baths, thoughtful storage, and layouts that don’t feel like “the leftover space.”

Most clients use these as guest suites, in-law units, or hybrid guest/rental spaces. We plan for daily life: where luggage goes, where coffee is made, and how you move at 2am.

Plumbing, handled without drama.

We coordinate utilities, plumbing layout, and permitting with your city. Our designs reflect Central Texas code realities, so we’re not promising layouts that won’t actually pass review.

We’ll walk through site access, tie-ins, and any constraints before you commit to a specific plan.

Thinking in Numbers

Suites that carry their own weight.

Many clients use Suite models as a combination of family guest space and income-generating short- or medium-term rentals. We’ll help you think through realistic rates and occupancy.

  • Identify what’s allowed on your property and in your city.
  • Estimate realistic nightly/weekly/monthly rates.
  • Look at rough payback periods based on different use patterns.
  • Compare Suite models to full ADUs for your goals.

Suite vs. ADU: where to start?

If you mostly need guest space and occasional rental income, a Suite is often the right call. When your priority is full-time rental or long-term housing, an ADU may make more sense.

Either way, we’ll walk through both options and run through high-level numbers in your consultation— no pressure, just clarity.
