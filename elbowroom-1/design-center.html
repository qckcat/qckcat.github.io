How It Works

From idea to a real number.

The Design Center is where we turn “I think I want an ADU” into a grounded, realistic plan. It’s not a final contract—it’s the clearest way to understand scope, finishes, and budget together.

Step 1

Choose structure type

Start with the big decision: Studio (no plumbing), Suite (with bath), or Residence (full ADU).

Step 2

Select a model

Pick a named floor plan—like The Mark, The Retreat, or The Compound— that fits your yard and goals.

Step 3

Dial in finishes

Explore curated finish packages and key upgrades. See how moving between tiers affects your overall range.

Step 4

Save & share

Save your configuration, export a summary, and send it to our team so we can review your property and city and refine the numbers together.

What the Design Center is (and isn’t).

The Design Center gives you structured choices and live price ranges based on our real builds in Central Texas.

  • ✓ Good for comparing Studio vs Suite vs Residence
  • ✓ Good for feeling out finish levels and big upgrades
  • ✓ Good for a ballpark range before site review

It doesn’t replace permits, engineering, or city approvals—we’ll layer those in once we’ve seen your lot, utilities, and local rules.

After the Configurator

What happens once you click “Save.”

Once you’ve built a configuration you like, our team reviews it against your actual property, city rules, and site constraints. From there, we refine the scope, schedule, and final pricing.

We’ll look at:

  • • Your chosen model and structure type (Studio / Suite / Residence)
  • • Major upgrades and finish level assumptions
  • • Site realities: access, topography, utilities
  • • City: permit path, ADU rules, parking, and setbacks

Send us your Design Center file.

When you’re ready, send your saved configuration to our team with a quick note about who you’re building for—tenant, family, or “we’re not sure yet.”

We’ll schedule a call, sanity-check the assumptions, and talk through the best path forward for your specific lot.
