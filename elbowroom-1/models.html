Models & Floor Plans

Three structure types.
One Elbow Room standard.

Start with the right structure for your property—an accessory structure, an accessory structure with plumbing, or a full ADU. From there, choose a model for how you actually live: quiet offices, guest suites, or complete backyard homes.

Pricing overview: Accessory Structures from the mid-$20s · Plumbing suites from the mid-$70s · Full ADUs up to ~$197k

Type 01

Accessory Structures

Finished, climate-controlled backyard spaces without plumbing—ideal for offices, studios, gyms, and workrooms that feel like part of the home, not an afterthought.

Best fits: Offices, art studios, music rooms, home gyms, workshops.

Jump to models →
Type 02

Accessory Structures w/ Plumbing

Suites with full bathrooms and optional kitchenettes—built for guest stays, aging parents, or rentals where comfort and privacy really matter.

Best fits: Guest casitas, in-law suites, long-term visitors, boutique rentals.

Jump to models →
Type 03

ADUs (Full Residences)

Backyard homes that live like a small house, with full kitchens and 1–2 bedrooms, designed for long-term rentals or multi-generational living.

Best fits: Full-time tenants, adult children, parents, or downsizing on your own lot.
