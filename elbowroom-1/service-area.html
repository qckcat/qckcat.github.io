Central Texas focus

We build where we can do our best work.

Elbow Room is based in Central Texas, and our projects reflect that: sun, heat, shade, privacy, and the specific mix of architectural styles in our region. When we say we understand your city’s process, it’s because we’ve lived it—multiple times.

Our core service area is built around three lanes:

  • • San Antonio – Primary base of operations, with deep familiarity across many neighborhoods.
  • • Austin – ADU-heavy environment, with specific zoning and utility coordination realities.
  • • Select Central Texas cities – Nearby municipalities where we can maintain our standards.

Within those zones, we design and build:

  • • Studios (Accessory Structures without plumbing)
  • • Suites (Accessory Structures with bathrooms)
  • • Residences (full ADUs with kitchen + bath)

Nearby & case-by-case

Sometimes the answer is “yes, with a closer look.”

Central Texas is full of edge cases: lots that sit at the edge of a city limit, properties in an ETJ, or neighborhoods where one side of the street is governed differently from the other. It’s a lot for homeowners to track—that’s part of why we exist.

In some areas, we’ll say “yes, we build there” right away. In others, it’ll be “let’s take a closer look” before we commit to a Studio, Suite, or Residence concept.

Examples of where we often work

  • • San Antonio and surrounding suburbs.
  • • Austin and select core/near-core neighborhoods.
  • • Growing Central Texas communities with ADU-friendly codes.

Examples of “let’s check first”

  • • Properties near changing ETJ boundaries.
  • • Lots with unusual access, easements, or floodplain overlays.
  • • HOA-controlled neighborhoods with strict architectural guidelines.

The best way to move from “maybe” to “yes” or “no” is a quick conversation with your address, how you want to use the space, and whether Studio, Suite, or Residence feels closest to your needs.

What we’ll do with your address.

When you reach out with your property info, we’ll:

  • • Confirm whether we currently build in your area.
  • • Flag any obvious red flags or constraints we see early.
  • • Outline a path to refine your Studio, Suite, or Residence plan.

If you’re outside our current service area, we’ll say so directly and won’t try to stretch into a zone where we can’t maintain our standards.
