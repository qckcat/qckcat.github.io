Not sure what to say?

Use these prompts when you reach out.

You don’t have to know the technical terms. Pick the card that sounds most like you and use that language in your message. We’ll help translate it into a clear plan.

Studio (no plumbing)

“We need more space for daily life.”

  • • “We’re both working from home and running out of room.”
  • • “I need a quiet therapy / coaching / creative space.”
  • • “We’d love a gym or studio that doesn’t live in the garage.”

Suite (with bath)

“We want to host or care for people better.”

  • • “Parents or in-laws may need to stay for longer stretches.”
  • • “We want a real guest space, not just a couch.”
  • • “We’re picturing a pool house or teen hangout with a bathroom.”

Residence (full ADU)

“We’re thinking long-term flexibility and income.”

  • • “We’d like rental income but want to keep things high quality.”
  • • “We want a place for adult kids or family to land.”
  • • “We want a small second home on the property as a long-term play.”
