Models · Studio Collection

Studio Collection.

Accessory structures without plumbing, designed to feel like part of your home—offices, studios, gyms, and workrooms that stay comfortable year-round and actually get used.

Structure type: Accessory Structures (no plumbing) · Typical range: $26k–$45k

When you don’t need a bathroom—but you do need real space.

These models are perfect when your lot, budget, or use-case doesn’t call for plumbing. You still get full electrical, HVAC, insulation, and the same Elbow Room finish standard as our ADUs.

Most clients use Studio models for focused work, creative practice, or fitness. We design around how you move through the space—from power placement to natural light.

What’s included, structurally.

Every Studio model includes site-built construction, foundation, insulation, full electrical, HVAC, and interior finish package. No shipping containers, no prefab shells to “figure out later.”

We’ll confirm exact scope, utilities, and pricing based on your property, city, and site conditions.
